    About me

    Greetings! I am an Associate Professor in School of Information Science and Engineering at Ocean University of China. I am fortunate to be one member at Prof. Junyu Dong's Vision Lab. My research interests include: transfer learning, deep learning, computer vision, and remote sensing.

    I received my Ph.D. degree from School of Computer Science and Engineering, at Beihang University in 2015. I received my B.S. degree in Software engineering from Chongqing University in 2008.
